# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community for everyone, regardless of experience, identity, background, or ability.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and constructive.
- Focus criticism on code, behavior, documentation, and evidence.
- Respect privacy and never post credentials, tokens, cookies, private URLs, private content, or personal information.
- Accept maintainers' decisions about project scope and safety boundaries.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing another person's private information without permission.
- Deliberately bypassing the project's public-source or credential safety boundary.
- Disruptive or deceptive behavior that makes collaboration unsafe.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, reject, or lock contributions and discussions that violate this policy. Serious or repeated violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban from project spaces.

Report conduct concerns privately through [ishikawa.co/contact](https://ishikawa.co/contact/). Do not include unnecessary private information.
